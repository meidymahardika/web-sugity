Planting 45,000 Mangrove Trees in Muara Gembong

November 11, 2023

Motivated by the current major global issue of CO2 gas emissions that continue to increase, as well as local environmental issues, namely abrasion in the coastal areas of Bekasi, a real form of activity is needed that can help the Environment, Biodiversity, and Socio-Economy of the surrounding community. One form of activity was carried out from TAB Group Indonesia (SUGITY, TTEC, RPT) which continued planting mangrove trees in Kampung Beting, Pantai Bahagia Village, Muaragembong District.
