Sugity Creatives
Established in 1995, is one of subsidiary companies of TOYOTA AUTO BODY JAPAN, a leading manufacturer of minivans, commercial vehicles, and sport utility vehicles such as Alphard, Land Cruiser, Lexus, Noah, etc. Our mission is to enrich people's lives through motor vehicles that meet customer expectation while at the same time responding to environmental issues. SUGITY CREATIVES’ core business is manufacturing of various automotive plastic parts (the biggest in South East Asia) and in 2012 started assembling one of TOYOTA brand vehicles (NAV1).
