Welcome

Sugity Creatives

Established in 1995, is one of subsidiary companies of TOYOTA AUTO BODY JAPAN, a leading manufacturer of minivans, commercial vehicles, and sport utility vehicles such as Alphard, Land Cruiser, Lexus, Noah, etc. Our mission is to enrich people's lives through motor vehicles that meet customer expectation while at the same time responding to environmental issues. SUGITY CREATIVES’ core business is manufacturing of various automotive plastic parts (the biggest in South East Asia) and in 2012 started assembling one of TOYOTA brand vehicles (NAV1).

Vehicle Business

As one of Sugity Business Pillars as vehicle assembly & conversion, Sugity was growing and became vehicle assembler for Kijang Krista (1996-2022) Toyota Dyna & Hino Dutro (1996-2009) and Toyota NAV1 (2012-2016). Starting from 2017 until now, we expand our business to vehicle conversion for Hiace Luxury, Fortuner CCV, Welcab Vehicle (Sienta & Voxy), Public Transport (Hiace & Granmax) and Hiace Medical Mover, etc and will continue to provide unique & high technology conversion vehicle based on customer needs. As part of Toyota Autobody Affiliate, QUALITY, SAFETY AND DURABILITY is embedded as DNA for all of our product.
